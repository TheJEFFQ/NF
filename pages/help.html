Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Login" button and select "Sign up" to create your account. You can use your email or sign up with your social media account.
Is it free to post ads?
Yes, posting basic ads is completely free. We also offer premium features for better visibility.
How do I contact a seller?
Click on any listing and use the "Contact Seller" or "Show Phone Number" buttons to get in touch with the seller.
